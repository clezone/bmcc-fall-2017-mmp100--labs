Jermaine Cole

Jermaine Cole, stage name J Cole, is a Rapper, Singer, Song Writer and a Record Producer. Born in Frankfurt, Hesse in West Germany. J Cole's primary choice of music is rap. J Cole has been a big influence on me since a young age, his life scenerios he speaks of in his music for me are very relatable.
